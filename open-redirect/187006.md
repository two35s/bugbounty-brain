# Open Redirect in a DoD website

### SUMMARY BY U.S. DEPT OF DEFENSE

An open redirect vulnerability was found on a Department of Defense website which may trick a web user into being redirected to fake websites which may steal data or credentials (usernames and passwords), or trick users into installing malware. adrianomarcmont was able to demonstrate this vulnerability by crafting a specially formatted URL.
