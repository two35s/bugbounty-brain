# Open redirect affecting  m.rockstargames.com/

### SUMMARY BY ROCKSTAR GAMES

In this report, the researcher identified an open redirect vulnerability on our Support site that impacted `m.rockstargames.com`, among other subdomains that were no longer actively being maintained. By addressing the problem on the Support site, we were able to prevent it from being further exploited on m.rockstargames.com and other subdomains.
