### VULNERABILITY INFORMATION

Hi,

2] Then just changed above url like this https://withinsecurity.com/wp-admin/ then it's redirecting to https://accounts.google.com/o/oauth2/auth?response_type=code&redirect_uri=https%3A%2F%2Fwithinsecurity.com%2Fwp-login.php&client_id=484006783216-3i2lf5d4bdga5a8lfmcap7cbq43obg4u.apps.googleusercontent.com&scope=openid+email+https%3A%2F%2Fwww.googleapis.com%2Fauth%2Fuserinfo.profile&access_type=online&approval_prompt=auto&state=cb04a91ac5%257Chttps%253A%252F%252Fwithinsecurity.com%252Fwp-admin%252F [ I'm authenticated in google that's why]
