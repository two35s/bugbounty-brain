# CSRF in seller-us.tiktok.com/profile/account-setting/delegation-login 

### SUMMARY BY TIKTOK

A Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF) vulnerability was found on a US TikTok Seller endpoint which could have resulted in the ability to enable or disable another seller account's delegation mode. We have confirmed resolution of this finding and thank @eye_ for reporting this to our team.
