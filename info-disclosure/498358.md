# Image Injection on /bully/anniversaryedition may lead to OAuth token theft.

### SUMMARY BY ROCKSTAR GAMES

In this report, the researcher identified an image injection issue on `www.rockstargames.com/bully/anniversaryedition` that could be combined with other vulnerabilities to result in sensitive token theft under certain conditions. We resolved the image injection issue, preventing this series of vulnerabilities from being exploitable.
