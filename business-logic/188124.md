# Violation of secure design principles on a DoD website

### SUMMARY BY U.S. DEPT OF DEFENSE

A DoD website was misconfigured in a manner that could have allowed an attacker to collect sensitive information about a user. @spam404 was able to demonstrate this vulnerability by crafting a specially formatted URL. Thank you for notifying us of this vulnerability!


### RESEARCHER SUMMARY
