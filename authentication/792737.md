# Password Reset Link not expiring after changing the email Leads To Account Takeover

### SUMMARY BY NORD SECURITY

The researcher has identified an issue in our password reset workflow where the password reset URL was not expiring correctly after the user has requested a password change

### RESEARCHER SUMMARY

1) Go to this website : https://ucp.nordvpn.com/lost-password
2) Enter your main account (main@main.com)
3) Go to main@main.com mail adress and copy the link
4) Open in your browser.
5) Now lets chat with your support to changing mail request
6) After great chat with Claudia and Marcus (Cheers!) They changed my email from "main@main.com" to "main2@main2.com"
7) Go to step 4 and Enter new password.
8) Password will be changed!
