# Access to the business emails of Rockstar Support agents through the support platform

### SUMMARY BY ROCKSTAR GAMES

In this report, the researcher identified a flaw in our Zendesk configuration on https://support.rockstargames.com that could allow a user to see the business email of the Support agent who responded to the user's ticket, as well as some other information that was not intended to be customer facing. This issue was resolved earlier this year when we overhauled our Support site.
